Arbitration/ ADR

Arbitration and other modes of dispute resolution like mediation, conciliation, negotiations, Lok Adalats etc. are considered much more efficient than the conventional process of litigation in today’s time for it is far less time consuming, cost effective and provides flexibility to the strict rules of procedure and technicalities. These alternate dispute resolution mechanisms have proven to be one of the most efficacious in resolving disputes, especially commercial disputes and disputes related to commercial contracts, property, construction, real estate and insurance. Organizations like Indian Council of Arbitration (ICA) and the International Centre for Alternate Dispute Resolution (ICADR) were established in India to effectively implement the alternate dispute resolution mechanisms to promote amicable and quick settlement of disputes.

We at Aggarwal and Associates have immense experience in international commercial arbitration as well as domestic arbitration, mediation and conciliation proceedings and settlement negotiations. We provide a wide spectrum of services aligning the legal strategy with the commercial and professional interests of our clients.

Our team provides comprehensive assistance through our team of arbitration attorneys, who have diverse and deep sector knowledge of dispute resolution, in the areas of infrastructure development, aviation, property and real estate, domestic and cross-border investments, media and entertainment, telecommunications, amongst other corporate and contractual.

Our Firm regularly represents and advises numerous clients under various rules such as United Nations Commission on International Trade Law (UNCITRAL) Arbitration Rules and International Chamber of Commerce (ICC) Rules of Arbitration. Additionally, we have rendered representation to our clients before the Court of Arbitration for Sport in a number of matters as well. The Firm is further well networked domestically as well as internationally and has collaborations with various local law firms and international arbitration law firms to strategically and efficiently safeguard and protect the rights of our clients across borders.
