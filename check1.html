ABOUT ANUJ AGGARWAL & COMPANY (AAC)

Anuj Aggarwal & Company (AAC) is a specialized litigation law firm. AAC fights cases in Supreme Court of India, Delhi High Court, Central Administrative Tribunal, Delhi School Tribunal, Labour Courts & Industrial Tribunals and all District Courts in Delhi. It specializes in service laws, labour & industrial laws, property laws, matrimonial cases, civil disputes and criminal trials.



AAC understands and appreciates the nobility of the legal profession. It believes that justice should not be denied to anyone due to poverty or financial reasons. AAC, therefore, fights several legal battles on completely pro-bono basis.
